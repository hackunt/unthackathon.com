Frequently Asked Questions

How long is HackUNT?

HackUNT 2019 is from Saturday April 6th to Sunday April 7th. Students will be hacking for 24 hours. Don't worry though, there will be plenty of energy drinks.

What is a Hackathon?

A Hackathon is a programming marathon, where teams of 1-4 put their ideas together to create something exciting in only 24 hours. It gives teams of creators a chance to turn their ideas into reality.

Who can attend?

To attend as a hacker, you need to be a student and at least 18 years old. Unfortunately we are unable to accept participants under the age of 18.

Travel reimbursements?

We are not offering travel reimbursements at this time, but could in the future. Check back at a later date for updates!

What about sleep?

We do not officially have a sleeping area, however there will be lounge areas to rest from your hard work! But who needs sleep when you have Highbrew?!

Who is accepted?

UNT students get automatic acceptance. Applicants from other schools will get randomly selected. Don't worry if you haven't heard back, we send out acceptances in waves.

Any rules?

All work must be done at HackUNT 2019. We also adhere by MLH's Code of Conduct, which you can find here.

What do I need to bring?

All you need is your student ID and anything you want to use in your hack!

Got more questions?

Reach out to us at officialhackunt@gmail.com with your questions!
