FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS HACKUNT?

It's a Hackathon in the University of North Texas, open to students around the world! We provide WiFi, power, and food while you and your team create something new!

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

A Hackathon is a programming marathon, where teams of 2-5 put their ideas together to create something exciting in only 36 hours. It gives teams of hackers a chance to turn their ideas into reality.

CAN I ATTEND?

To attend as a hacker, you need to be a student and 18 years of age or older. Apologies, but we are unable to accept participants under the age of 18..

TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENTS?

We are not offering travel reimbursements at this time, but could in the future. Check back at a later date or message the team at OfficalHackUNT@gmail.com regarding your issue!

WHAT ABOUT SLEEP?

We do not officially have a sleeping area, however there will be lounge areas to rest from your hard work! But who needs sleep when you have Highbrew?!

HOW DO I REGISTER?

We usually release tickets in rounds. Check back later for more info!

HOW DO I WATCH THE LIVESTREAM?

Our live site will be available during the event at live.unthackathon.com!
