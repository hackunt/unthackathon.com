FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS HACKUNT?

It's a Hackathon in the University of North Texas, open to students around the world! We provide WiFi, power, and food while you and your team create something new.

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

A Hackathon is a programming marathon, where teams of 2-4 put their ideas together to create something exciting in only 24 hours. It gives teams of hackers a chance to turn their ideas into reality.

CAN I ATTEND?

To attend as a hacker, you need to be a university student or have left university in the past year. Apologies, but we are unable to accept participants under the age of 18..

TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENTS?

We are not offering travel reimbursements at this time, but could in the future. Check back at a later date or message the team at OfficalHackUNT@gmail.com regarding your issue!

WHAT ABOUT SLEEP?

We have a limited amount of space for sleeping with a limited amount of sleeping supplies. We recommend bringing sleep gear like a sleeping bag, pillow, or blanket.

WHAT DO I NEED TO BRING?

Your ticket, Student ID and a laptop with its charger. That's about it!

HOW DO I REGISTER?

We usually release tickets in rounds. Check back later for more info!

HOW DO I WATCH THE LIVESTREAM?

Go to URL and select Livestream from the navigation menu located at the top of our website
